# Partner Targets

## Integration Roadmap

### 1. Stripe API
- **Rationale**: Stripe is a leading payment processor with a robust API that supports global payments. Integrating with Stripe will allow Payment-Cortex to leverage its existing infrastructure and security measures.
- **Free-tier limits**: $20/month, 100 transactions.
- **Integration effort**: Medium (M)
- **Value-add**: Enables seamless payment processing for users, solving the job of handling transactions efficiently.
- **Affiliate/revenue-share**: Yes, Stripe offers affiliate and revenue-share programs.

### 2. Plaid API
- **Rationale**: Plaid provides access to a wide range of financial institutions, enabling Payment-Cortex to support a broad spectrum of bank accounts and payment methods.
- **Free-tier limits**: 100 transactions/month.
- **Integration effort**: Large (L)
- **Value-add**: Facilitates account aggregation and payment initiation, solving the job of managing multiple bank accounts and payment methods.
- **Affiliate/revenue-share**: Yes, Plaid offers affiliate and revenue-share programs.

### 3. Twilio SendGrid
- **Rationale**: SendGrid is a reliable email delivery service that can be used to send transactional emails, such as payment confirmations and receipts.
- **Free-tier limits**: 100 emails/day.
- **Integration effort**: Small (S)
- **Value-add**: Ensures reliable email delivery, solving the job of keeping users informed about their transactions.
- **Affiliate/revenue-share**: Yes, SendGrid offers affiliate and revenue-share programs.

### 4. AWS Payment Cryptography
- **Rationale**: AWS Payment Cryptography provides a secure and scalable infrastructure for handling sensitive payment data, ensuring compliance with global payment security standards.
- **Free-tier limits**: 12 months free trial.
- **Integration effort**: Large (L)
- **Value-add**: Enhances security and compliance, solving the job of protecting sensitive payment data.
- **Affiliate/revenue-share**: No, but AWS offers a free trial.
