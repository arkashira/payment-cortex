| **Expand** | • Business unit requests additional rails (e.g., crypto‑to‑fiat settlement) after seeing cost savings | • Need to scale to new geographies, currencies, and regulatory regimes <br>• Managing increased transaction volume and new compliance rules | Excited, growth‑focused, looking for partnership depth | • Introduce a “Cortex Marketplace” where customers can enable new adapters (e.g., Central Bank Digital Currencies) with a single click <br>• Quarterly Business Review that surfaces incremental ROI (e.g., $ M saved) and suggests next‑step modules (advanced fraud‑ML, dynamic pricing) <br>• Loyalty program: volume‑based discounts and early‑access to beta features | **Net Revenue Expansion Rate** – % increase in ARR from existing customers (goal ≥ 30 % YoY) |

### Narrative Flow (quick read)

1. **Aware** – Executives discover the hidden cost of current cross‑border pipelines → we capture attention with a fast ROI calculator and crisp video.  
2. **Consider** – Ops evaluates integration depth → we lower perceived risk with ready‑made SDKs, compliance docs, and a fast‑track integration sprint.  
3. **Try** – A sandbox proves performance at scale → we deliver a production‑grade test environment and a dedicated success engineer.  
4. **Adopt** – Contract signed, migration begins → we guarantee zero‑downtime migration, live war‑room support, and a first‑month credit.  
5. **Expand** – Success fuels demand for more rails and features → we open a marketplace, run ROI‑focused QBRs, and reward volume growth.
