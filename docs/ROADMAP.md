# ROADMAP.md
## Introduction
The payment-cortex project aims to deliver a modern, scalable, and secure infrastructure for global payments. This roadmap outlines the key milestones and phases for the project, ensuring a clear direction and focus on delivering a high-quality product.

## MVP Milestone
The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) milestone is crucial for the launch of payment-cortex. The following features are considered MVP-critical:
### Must-Have Features
* **Secure Payment Processing**: Implement a secure payment processing system that supports multiple payment methods and currencies.
* **Cross-Border Transaction Support**: Enable efficient and reliable cross-border transactions for banks, payment providers, corporations, and digital asset exchanges.
* **Scalable Architecture**: Design a scalable architecture that can handle a high volume of transactions and support future growth.
* **Compliance and Regulatory Framework**: Ensure compliance with relevant regulations and standards, such as anti-money laundering (AML) and know-your-customer (KYC).
* **Basic User Interface**: Develop a basic user interface for payment providers, corporations, and digital asset exchanges to manage their transactions.

## v1 Phase
The v1 phase will focus on building a robust and feature-rich product. The following themes and features are planned:
### Theme: Payment Method Expansion
* **Additional Payment Methods**: Integrate support for additional payment methods, such as credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets.
* **Currency Support**: Expand currency support to include more international currencies.
### Theme: User Experience Enhancements
* **Advanced User Interface**: Develop an advanced user interface with improved usability and features, such as transaction tracking and reporting.
* **Mobile Support**: Ensure mobile support for the user interface, enabling users to manage transactions on-the-go.
### Theme: Security and Compliance
* **Advanced Security Features**: Implement advanced security features, such as two-factor authentication and encryption.
* **Regulatory Compliance**: Ensure compliance with evolving regulatory requirements and standards.

## v2 Phase
The v2 phase will focus on innovation and differentiation. The following themes and features are planned:
### Theme: Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
* **AI-Powered Payment Processing**: Integrate artificial intelligence and machine learning to improve payment processing efficiency and accuracy.
* **Predictive Analytics**: Develop predictive analytics capabilities to enable users to forecast and optimize their transactions.
### Theme: Blockchain and Digital Assets
* **Blockchain Integration**: Explore the integration of blockchain technology to enable secure and transparent transactions.
* **Digital Asset Support**: Expand support for digital assets, such as cryptocurrencies and tokens.
### Theme: Partnerships and Integrations
* **Strategic Partnerships**: Establish strategic partnerships with key players in the payment industry to expand the reach and capabilities of payment-cortex.
* **API and Integration Framework**: Develop an API and integration framework to enable seamless integration with other systems and platforms.

## Conclusion
The payment-cortex roadmap is designed to deliver a modern, scalable, and secure infrastructure for global payments. By focusing on MVP-critical features, followed by themed phases, we can ensure a clear direction and focus on delivering a high-quality product that meets the needs of our users.
